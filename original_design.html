Baseline modeling - Forecasting - Collaboration Built by hydrologists for hydrologists

Motivation

This project aims to address the challenges in hydrology by establishing standardized benchmarks and common metrics to assess and demonstrate advancements in hydrologic forecasting, tackling issues like model irreproducibility and the impact of isolated academic findings. We want to enable quick exploratory evaluation in addition to formal (protocol) evaluations.

Goal

We're crafting a user-friendly web-based testbed for hydrological prediction scientists. Centered around collaborative modeling protocols, teams can swiftly adjust models, improving hydrologic forecast accuracy. Our platform fosters collaboration and provides interactive validation, driving breakthroughs in forecast science.

Workflow

Protocols are the heart of the testbed. The testbed allows for sharing protocols, quickly creating protocols, adjusting them and seeing the results.
